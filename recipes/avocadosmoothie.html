Avocado Smoothie

This is an image of the delicious avocado smoothie made from this recipe.

Thick, creamy, and delicious. Makes a great summertime smoothie.

Ingredients (2 servings):

  • 1 ripe avocado, halved and pitted
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vanilla yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 8 ice cubes

Steps:

  1. Combine the avocado, milk, yogurt, honey, and ice cubes in a blender; blend until smooth.
  2. That is it! The recipe is quick and easy :)
